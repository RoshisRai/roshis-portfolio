# About

## Who I Am

I am a full-stack software developer based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, with experience building production applications across freelance, contract, and personal projects.

My work sits at the intersection of modern full-stack engineering and practical AI integration. I enjoy building systems that are fast, scalable, maintainable, and genuinely useful to real users.

I approach software engineering as both a technical and product discipline — not just writing code, but designing systems that solve meaningful problems reliably over time.

## Background

I started by building full-stack Django applications for real clients in Nepal across industries including construction, publishing, e-commerce, agriculture, and Web3.

Those early projects gave me experience working directly with clients, shipping under deadlines, handling production data, and making technical decisions independently.

As I continued my education at Seneca Polytechnic in Canada, I expanded deeper into the modern JavaScript and TypeScript ecosystem, focusing on React, Next.js, Node.js, backend architecture, and AI-integrated systems.

Over time, my interests shifted increasingly toward:
- scalable backend systems
- AI-powered user experiences
- semantic search
- automation tooling
- modern frontend engineering
- developer experience

## How I Build

I enjoy working across the entire stack.

On the backend side, I like designing APIs, database schemas, workflow systems, authentication layers, and infrastructure that remain maintainable as products grow.

On the frontend side, I care deeply about interaction quality, performance, animation, accessibility, and building interfaces that feel polished and responsive.

I also enjoy integrating AI into products in ways that feel structurally useful rather than superficial. My focus is usually on building AI directly into workflows, search systems, automation pipelines, and product behavior rather than treating it as an isolated feature.
